Andrew Ross Sorkin, author of Too Big to Fail


November 2015

New York Times columnist and author, Andrew Ross Sorkin, has been describe as, "the most famous financial journalist of his

generation. "His book, Too Big to Big Fail" was the first behind-the-scene of the financial crisis that lead to our current recession.


The Economist, The Financail Times, Business Week all named Too Big to Fail one of the best book of the year. The book was

published by Viking October 20, 2009. The was adapted as a amovie by HBO films and a premiere on HBO May 23 2011.
